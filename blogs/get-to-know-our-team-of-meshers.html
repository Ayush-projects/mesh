Meet the people driving performance management transformation

Meet the team behind Mesh. We have some of the industry's smartest & most experienced people working together to transform performance management.

It's been over a year and we thought it be best, to introduce you to our incredible team of Meshers who've made it all possible for us!

Gaurav Suman

Gaurav Suman our team’s Tony Stark. He’s always busy designing and developing amazing super interactive UI for users. He’s a BTech CSE graduate from IIT Patna and has worked as a software engineer (in both data engineering and frontend engineering roles) at Sigmoid in Bengaluru.

Outside of work he keeps a lot of interests! He enjoys solving chess puzzles, watching tv series, and anime. Occasionally he’ll like to read blogs, and both tech and non tech related articles.

For Gaurav, Mesh (YC S20) is an opportunity to learn, create, contribute and grow

Pranav Mankar

Pranav Mankar who’s much like the Doctor from Doctor Who. He’s always looking for new challenges and innovative ways to overcome obstacles.

He’s done his engineering from Pune University and has an MBA from IIM Indore. Prior to Mesh, he worked at Snapdeal and Hike! Pranav is definitely the go to guy! Outside of work, he likes to play cricket, badminton and hanging back to play FIFA. For Pranav, Mesh is a place to express himself.

Abhay Singh

Our very own Saul Goodman. He’s always hustling, gives tough love, is amazing at problem solving and is funny as hell!

He’s done his bachelors in business studies from CBS, DU and has previously worked at Aon and Korn Ferry before joining us at Mesh. In his free time you can see him watching drama series and movies or Liverpool games ⚽️. He also likes reading about human behavior, politics and science.

Sakshi Arora

Our very own Hermione Granger, who similar to her loves gaining knowledge by reading and uses her new knowledge when the appropriate time arises.

Sakshi has been pivotal to making Mesh, and because of that, we’re like her baby. She did her BTech from NIT Kurukshetra and has previously worked at Amazon, TargetingMantra and DataMonk. She’s worked on various personalisation and communication platforms and gained experience working on high scale traffic. A huge movie buff outside work you’ll find her binge watching sitcoms or Bollywood movies.

Saurabh Banka

Next, there's Saurabh Banka, our very own Jean-Luc Picard who’s constantly adapting to situations at hand and makes informed decisions.

He graduated from MNNIT Allahabad with a major in civil engineering and love for computers. Since then he’s worked across India and the US building fintech software for hedge funds and financial firms before joining Mesh. An outdoorsy guy, when he’s not on his system you’ll find him either running or hiking. For him Mesh is an opportunity to collaborate, contribute, learn and grow!

Vikalp Sajwan

Vikalp Sajwan is our very own Jim from The Office who is super funny and kind. He’s always been a tech enthusiast since his early school days and is always amazed by how rapidly technology is changing. This passion resulted in him getting a BTech in Computer Science.

Being an admirer of details, he developed a passion for creating beautiful and pleasant to use front end products. Which brought him to Mesh, which came as a “timely opportunity” for him.

When Vikalp isn’t busy making our lives easier at Mesh, you’ll find him cranking up some tunes. For him, modern Bollywood classical beings the lyrics, vocals and instrumentals. He also enjoys going trekking once a year, and loves running!

Aman Sharma

Aman Sharma is our UI extraordinaire, or Batman here at Mesh He’s got a softer side, tries not to kill, and would never want a user to reach a dead end in a product.

He has done his masters in Design from NIFT (Mumbai). Since then he’s worked for a range of companies like Magicbricks and Quikr but felt he wanted to to solve more complex issues and joined our fam at Mesh.

Aman loves to stay active and on the weekdays you’ll generally find him hitting the gym. Come Friday night he’ll either be playing football or hanging with his crew. All that activity needs him to get his rest but he’s not lazy. He wants you to know - HE. ISN’T. LAZY. OKAY?

Aman sees working at Mesh, both a challenge and an opportunity to solve complex problems. We’re sure he’ll rise to every occasion, after all, he’s Batman.

Arun Kumar

Meet our tech backend wiz, Arun Kumar. He’s basically the Ash Ketchum of our Pokeverse here at Mesh. He’s always dreaming big of where he wants to be, strives to do his best and makes sure he helps everyone with him along the way.

He has a master’s degree in information technology from IIIT-H University with a specialisation in Data Analysis. Arun is for sure an OG in the game. Prior to joining our fam at Mesh, he also worked along side our co-founders at their previous startup, Datamonk for a year. He is the go to guy at Mesh for any Spark related discussions or resolving any critical client issues.

He’s a man of many interests which a pretty varied if you ask us! It ranges from cooking up a yum storm in the kitchen to a love for rajneeti, or politics (the dirtier the better for Arun). When he’s not glued to the TV staying updated with politics, he’s probably enjoying some sports. He keeps a keen interest in cricket, football or soccer, chess and table tennis.

Most of all, he loves hiking. He often says, “the best view comes after the hardest climb” and that’s a great way to describe his journey till now at Mesh in a way. It’s been hectic, but definitely worth it.
